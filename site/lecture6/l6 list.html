40％ rye with walnut & raisin

recipe
  • 60% bread flour
  • 40% rye flour
  • 20% rye starter
  • 74% hydration
  • 2% salt
  • 18% walnut&raisin
method
  1. Autolyse 30min
  2. mix&knead until smooth
  3. bf in proofer until volumn increase 30%(about 3.5hr, ddt 30c)
  4. bench rest 30mins
  5. shape and cold proofing about 12hr
  6. bake 230c with steam 20min without steam 35min.
